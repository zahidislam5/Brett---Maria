Our Story

Our Story

He who finds a wife finds a good thing, And obtains favor from the LORD. Proverbs 18:22

  • God is faithful. Through our obedience, service, and surrender, our Lord Jesus fulfilled His promise of a life partner. Brett and I were introduced in early May 2022 by a servant of God that met both of us separately during the previous two weeks. She was prompted by the Holy Spirit to introduce us through our common request, "looking for someone who loves Jesus!" The rest is history.

  • Since Brett lives in FL and Maria is in NYC, Brett booked his trip to NYC a few days after speaking on the phone. Our immeasurably more God brought Brett to Maria’s doorstep. Dinner, hiking, Central Park, a Christian concert, the beach, and much more over several days is how it all began.

  • Our favorite dating memories include praying for each other from the very beginning, countless Holy Spirit confirmations that God had brought us together, and our first event together in October that has already made an impact on many lives.

  • After only five months (that felt like more than a year together), Brett proposed in front of Maria’s church, Life Center NYC. On February 27, 2023, we will begin our covenant marriage surrounded by close friends and family as witnesses.

What to Wear

What to Wear

The wedding is not large so we intend to get pictures with everyone. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • This is not going to be a black tie affair and the event is in South Florida, however, everyone should be dressed appropriately.
  • The city dock is windy and also not ideal for thin high heels so keep that in mind ladies.
  • Suit and tie are welcomed but not required.
  • Things to avoid are t-shirts, shorts, jeans, and bright colors such as pinks, oranges, yellows and reds.
  • If you are not sure what colors will be best for the event, neutral colors (without floral patterns or designs) that blend with these colors will be great!
